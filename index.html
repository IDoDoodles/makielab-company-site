What do we do?

MakieLab is a London-based startup manufacturing game-connected smart toys, with stereotype-breaking, creative values.

Founded by Alice Taylor, Jo Roach and Sulka Haro in late 2011, MakieLab launched their first product Makies as a demo in summer of 2013.

Martha Lane Fox is Chair of MakieLab, and Tech City Insider calls MakieLab “one of London’s most admired tech startups”. Wired UK included MakieLab at #3 of Europe’s Hottest 100 Startups.

MakieLab is HQ’d near Brick Lane, in London’s Shoreditch.
